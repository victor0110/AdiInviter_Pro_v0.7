Installation Instructions.

  1. Upload contents of folder Upload to your vBulletin forum root folder.
  2. Go to your Admincp -> Plugins & Products -> Manage Products.

    • Click on [Add/Import Product].
    • Browse to the Product XML folder and open adiinviter_pro_lite_07.xml, click Import.
  3. Go to your Admincp -> AdiInviter Pro-Lite (On the left panel) -> AdiInviter Pro Settings, and configure AdiInviter Pro v0.7 Lite.

Uninstallation Instructions.

  1. Go to your Admincp -> Plugins & Products -> Manage Products.

    • Look for product AdiInviter Pro v0.7 Lite and select Uninstall from the controls dropdown menu.
  2. Remove following files from your FTP -

    • your_forum_root/adiinviter.php
    • your_forum_root/includes/xml/cpnav_adiinviter_settings.xml
    • Remove folder : your_forum_root/images/adiinviter
